2025 Year in Review

2025 was a big year for the League of Legends Wiki, and honestly, a pretty fun one too. It marked our first full year since the fork from Weird Gloop, and with that came a mix of fresh starts, growing pains, and plenty of wins. From building our own foundation to experimenting, improving, and expanding coverage, this year was all about figuring out who we are as an independent wiki and leaning into it. Let's take a look back at how it all went.

Traffic statistics

The wiki continued to grow in popularity, with over 95 million total pageviews over the course of the year. Traffic was relatively consistent, with notable peaks around the starts of each season and during the World Championship.
